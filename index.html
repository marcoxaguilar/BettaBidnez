How much does furnace repair cost in Gurnee, IL? +
Furnace repair costs in Gurnee typically range from $150 to $500 depending on the issue. Common repairs like ignitor replacement or blower motor fixes are on the lower end, while heat exchanger problems cost more. We offer free estimates so you know exactly what to expect — no hidden fees. Call us at (224) 656-5843 for a personalized quote.
How often should I schedule a furnace or AC tune-up? +
We recommend scheduling your furnace tune-up in early fall (September–October) and your AC tune-up in spring (April–May). Annual maintenance prevents up to 95% of breakdowns, improves energy efficiency by 10–15%, and can extend your system's lifespan by several years.
What HVAC services does Better Climate Zone offer? +
We offer furnace repair, installation, and maintenance; air conditioning repair, installation, and tune-ups; boiler installation; humidifier and thermostat installation; and professional duct and vent cleaning. We service all major HVAC brands for residential and commercial customers across Lake County, IL.
What areas in Lake County do you serve? +
We proudly serve Gurnee, Grayslake, Round Lake, Highland Park, Glenview, Palatine, Wheeling, Northbrook, Fox Lake, Hoffman Estates, Des Plaines, Algonquin, and Kenosha, WI. We cover all of Lake County, IL and surrounding areas.
Do you offer free estimates for HVAC work? +
Yes! We offer completely free, no-obligation estimates for all services. You'll know exactly what your project costs before any work begins — no hidden fees, no surprise charges. Call (224) 656-5843 or fill out our form to get started.
When should I replace my furnace or AC unit? +
Most furnaces last 15–20 years and AC units 12–15 years. Consider replacement if your system is over 15 years old, requires frequent repairs, has rising energy bills, heats/cools unevenly, or uses R-22 refrigerant. Our team provides honest recommendations on whether repair or replacement makes more financial sense.
